Hamburgers

By Anon

Description

prep: 10 mins cook: 10 mins total: 20 mins Servings: 5 Yield: 5 servings

You can use ground turkey in place of beef, if you like. Nutrition Facts Per Serving: 472 calories; protein 29g; carbohydrates 43.8g; fat 19.3g; cholesterol 73.9mg; sodium 1802.8mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 packet of ranch seasoning
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • Â½ onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 5 slices of cheese(Optional)
  • 1 package of hamburger buns

Directions

  1. Step 1: Mix ingredients together and roll into balls slightly smaller than a baseball.
  2. Step 2: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook balls in pan by smashing them down with a spatula or burger press. 4-5 minutes a side.
  3. Step 3: If desired add cheese slice
  4. Step 4: Patties are done when they are brown on the outside. 4-5 minutes a side, depending on doneness.
  5. Step 5: Place on bun.
