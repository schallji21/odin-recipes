Basic Baked Chicken

Description

prep: 10 mins cook: 35 mins total: 45 mins Servings: 3 Yield: 3 servings

Nutrition Facts Per Serving: 472 calories; protein 29g; carbohydrates 43.8g; fat 19.3g; cholesterol 73.9mg; sodium 1802.8mg.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

  1. Step 1: Wash and dry chicken breasts
  2. Step 2: Place chicken on foil or greased pan.
  3. Step 3: Place pan in oven at 375 degrees for 35 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Check chicken for doneness> No pink should be seen when cutting chicken in half.
